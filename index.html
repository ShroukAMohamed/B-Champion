About Us

B Champion sports is a swimming academy established in 2020 in Dubai. The Academy specializes in individual and group swimming lessons for children and adults

Our Vision :-

We are committed to being an industry leader through all aspects of our business, achieving excellence in providing swimming services. B Champions sports services is dedicated to the creation of an environment which fosters happiness and learning through creativity, innovation and helping all the swimmers reach their personal goals

Coaches

Mostafa Ali

Founder And Head Coach

Mostafa holds an ASA Levels 1 to 5 qualifications. He has done Foundations of Coaching School Level 1 and Coaching Dry Land for All Swimmers at American Swimming Coaches Assosiation which is accredited by the UAE Swimming Federation & Professional Sports & Lifeguard Services. He is equipped with CPR training and other first aid methods. He has over 14 years of experience as a swimming coach both in Egypt and UAE. With his knowledge and abilities, he is capable of providing lessons to various age groups. He is passionate about swimming and coaching which led him to establishing B Champion Sports Services, to provide quality lessons to people who want to learn the skills of swimming.

Diaa Mahmoud

Diaa is a Bachelor of Physical Education graduate and has attended various courses such as Lifesaving and first aid, Swimming coach preparation, Sport injuries, and Hydrotherapy and kinesio tape. He has 9 years of Experience in sports-related work. He was a physical education teacher in a national school and has been a swimming coach to multiple swimming academies. Diaa is one of the most commended coaches in our team. Based on client's feedbacks, students can easily go along with him, and he teaches in a way that they can learn while they are having fun and great time during each session.

Karim Ali

Karim has Physical Education background. He has been a swimming trainer for over 11 years now. He is also into fitness and diving. He had done seminars related to emergency first response, lifesaving federation in first aid and rescuing. He also has PADI Divemaster certificate, ACE's personal training program design, and ISSA's personal trainer certificate. Karim was the Assistant Fitness Coach of Egypt's National Swimming Team. He gained a lot of experiences that help him be a professional and qualified swimming instructor.
